# Project Overview: Unimeds

## 1. Vision & Intent
Unimeds is a next-generation, premium AI-powered patient engagement and healthcare management platform. It transforms fragmented healthcare interactions into a unified, high-end ecosystem by bridging the gap between patients, doctors, and clinic administrators. The application acts as a premium digital layer optimizing workflows through automated scheduling, ultra-fast AI OCR document parsing, and local voice orchestration.

## 2. Core Stakeholder Experience
* **Patient Portal:** A luxurious, friction-free interface for managing personal health timelines, secure medical record storage, multi-channel family sub-profiles, and elegant appointment wizard flows.
* **Doctor Workspace:** A highly optimized clinical cockpit providing omni-search capabilities, rapid patient profile context switching, and structured OCR document insight grids.
* **Clinic Administration:** A clean operational nerve center offering live appointment queues, multi-tenant scheduling grids, and high-end visual data reports.

## 3. Product Principles
* **Design as a Feature:** Exceptional aesthetic execution directly establishes trust in sensitive clinical environments.
* **Invisible Utility:** Complex background processing (AI processing, OCR parsing, multi-agent flows) must feel instantaneous and fluid via high-performing optimistic UI states and skeleton placeholders.
* **Absolute Integrity:** Built with defensive data engineering, end-to-end role validation, and immutable logging across every action.
